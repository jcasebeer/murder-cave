MURDER CAVE

  • High Concept:
    You are a cop going lone wolf investigating a cave full of murderers...
    a murder cave!
  • Player Experience Goals:
    Players should have fun for 1 to 5 minutes
  • Genre: platformer arcade game.
  • Game play: The player can move around and shoot their gun in any of the four cardinal directions.
    The player can use their gun to shoot the cave walls to make a path for themselves.
    The player's gun is also their primary tool to take out the murderers! use it wisely!
    The player can move onward to the next procedurally generated level by getting to the bottom of the screen
    The game has no sort of score system, the death rattle of each violent murderer should be
    enough satisfaction to anyone who plays this game.
  • Prototype goal:
    Randomly generated caves
    using an array to store level data
  • Features: randomly generated levels
    fully destructable levels!
  • Setting/Premise: You are a cop investigating a cave which sources say is full of murderers.
    As you peer into the darkness you realize "Oh Gee, it's true!"
  • Story: N/A
  • Target Audience: The game is targeted at people who like digging tunnels and dislike murderers.
  • Play time: forever until you die! When you do die you'll find yourself once again at the opening of
    the cave... no this isn't groundhog day. I just was too tired to implement a proper
    game over screen.
  • Strategies:
    Shoot a path across before shooting down to maximize the number of murderers
    you can take out on each screen.
  • Prototype Assets: I made all of the assets except for the sound murderers make when they die.
